PCPhone allows you to control your PC with any device that supports a browser.

What is PCPhone ?

This is the official website of PCPhone.

PCPhone allows you to automate simple things you do every day with you phone on your computer. Like open link, folder, file, execute software, macro, all that you want with your any device.

Whether you are a developer, a designer, a simple person, a company, PCPhone is make for you.

Besides, PCPhone is open source, no ads, no traking, and completly free.

How to install PCPhone ?

For the pc

You need to download and install the last version of PCPhone ( on GitHub )

After, you need to start the software, and it's over for your pc.

You can test it by open this link : http://localhost:8080

For the phone ( or another device )

You need to open your favour browser and write your
"http://your-ip-adresse:8080"

You can see directly your ip adressse on the PCPhone application
(here is an example)

And PCPhone will be ready to use !
